About Me

Hi! My name is Elizabeth and I am currently a high school senior at American Heritage Plantation. I am the co-editor in chief and photo editor of the Spotlight Yearbook staff at our school. As a self-taught photographer, I love to look for the little details that most people don't notice through my camera and capture the beauty of shadows, light, and people’s emotions. I recently interned at Little’s photography where I got background experience in working with professional photographers and learning about how they engage with their customers to give them the best experience and photos possible. On my free time I enjoy cooking, exercising, and playing the piano. I hope you enjoy some snapshots of the experiences and moments I were privileged to capture!
